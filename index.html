A Victorian conversion for a Latimer Road local

The building is a smart brick-fronted Victorian conversion, nestled in a peaceful surrounding area between White City and North Kensington. With industrial features and a bright fresh interior, the building has 5 privately newly-refurbished offices ready to move into.

An adaptable space
to make your own

These suites are ideal for a local occupier looking for a smart space ready to make their own. Park House is ideal for those who want to avoid the commute to the City and drive to the office instead, with parking available on-site too.

Designed to inspire

All included in one simple fixed monthly price, you get to customise the space by partnering with our interior design team and also have it managed for you by your Kitt Space Partner so that you can focus on your business.
